PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated:

Foodie is a food ordering website that aims to provide users with a seamless experience for ordering food online, taking orders for pickup, and booking tables for dining in. The website showcases the quality and specialties of the food offered, along with a variety of services to cater to different user preferences.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including, but not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, and payment details. This information is necessary for processing orders, improving our services, and enhancing your experience on our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected in various ways, including:

  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Improving our products and services
  • Communicating with you about your orders
  • Sending promotional emails and offers (with your consent)

Security of Your Information

We implement security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access or alteration and ensure its confidentiality. Our security practices are regularly reviewed and enhanced to safeguard your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.

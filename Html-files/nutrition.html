Food Groups

Fruits

Rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

High in dietary fiber which aids digestion and prevents constipation. Low in calories and fat, making them ideal for weight management. Provides essential nutrients like vitamin C, potassium, and folate.

Vegetables

Packed with vitamins, minerals, and phytonutrients.

High in fiber which supports digestive health and helps control blood sugar levels. Low in calories and fats, contributing to overall weight management. Contains antioxidants that help reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and cancer.

hover me Read More

Grains

Good source of carbohydrates, which provide energy.

Whole grains are high in fiber, aiding in digestion and keeping you full longer. Contains essential nutrients like B vitamins, iron, magnesium, and selenium. Helps maintain healthy cholesterol levels and reduce the risk of heart disease.

Proteins

Essential for building and repairing tissues, including muscles.

Provides important nutrients such as iron, zinc, and B vitamins. Supports immune function and hormone production. Helps in maintaining a healthy weight by promoting satiety and reducing overall calorie intake.

Dairy

Excellent source of calcium, which is crucial for bone health.

Provides protein, vitamin D, potassium, and phosphorus. Supports healthy teeth and prevents osteoporosis. Contributes to muscle function and nerve signaling.

Fats and Oils

Provides essential fatty acids that the body cannot produce on its own.

Aids in the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, and K). Supports cell growth, brain function, and the production of hormones. Healthy fats (such as those from avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil) can reduce the risk of heart disease.

Ketogenic Diet

A high-fat, low-carbohydrate diet that forces the body to burn fats rather than carbohydrates for energy. It is used to help manage epilepsy and promote weight loss.

Mediterranean Diet

Emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, legumes, nuts, and healthy fats like olive oil. It is inspired by the dietary patterns of countries bordering the Mediterranean Sea and is associated with improved cardiovascular health.

Paleo Diet

Focuses on consuming foods that would have been available to our Paleolithic ancestors, such as lean meats, fish, fruits, vegetables, nuts, and seeds. It excludes processed foods, grains, and dairy.

Gluten-Free Diet

Eliminates all foods containing gluten, a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. It is essential for individuals with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

Low-FODMAP Diet

Restricts foods high in certain fermentable carbohydrates (FODMAPs) to alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS). Foods like certain fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and grains are limited.

Intermittent Fasting

Involves alternating periods of eating and fasting. Common methods include the 16/8 method (16 hours of fasting and an 8-hour eating window) and the 5:2 method (eating normally for 5 days and reducing calorie intake on 2 non-consecutive days).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I place an order? ▼

To place an order, browse our menu, select the items you want, and add them to your cart. Once you’re ready, click on the cart icon and proceed to checkout. Follow the instructions to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and popular digital wallets. You can select your preferred payment method at the checkout page.

Can I schedule an order for later? ▼

Yes, you can schedule an order for a later time. During checkout, select the delivery time that suits you best.

How can I track my order? ▼

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS. You can use this link to track the status of your delivery in real-time.

What should I do if I have a problem with my order? ▼

If you encounter any issues with your order, please contact our customer support immediately. You can reach us via the contact form on our website, email, or phone. We’re here to help you!

Is there a minimum order value? ▼

Yes, there is a minimum order value depending on your location. The minimum order amount will be displayed during the checkout process.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions? ▼

We regularly offer discounts and promotions. Check our website or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals and offers.
